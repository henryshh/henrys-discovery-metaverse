## 🧠 Lessons Learned
- **Naming by Function**: In ML projects, naming UI components by their *technique* (e.g., CNN) is less resilient than naming them by their *purpose* (e.g., Prediction).
- **Term Choice**: "Metaverse" vs "Universe" - "Metaverse" conveys the digital-first, interactive nature of 3D data exploration more effectively in this context.
